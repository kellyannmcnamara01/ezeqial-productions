About

Ezeqial Productions is an award-winning Canadian production company that focuses on original stories with unique points-of-view. Founded by Zack Bernbaum, Ezeqial offers creative thinking with a practical approach.

Our films have played theatrically and sold internationally, and our latest film, The Dancing Dogs Of Dombrova, had its World Premiere at the Austin Film Festival and Canadian Premiere at the Whistler Film Festival. It will be released in North America by Film Movement.

We are in development on a number of exciting projects including CUT FOR STONE, which finished in the Top 50 of the Academy’s Nicholl Fellowship in Screenwriting.

Feel free to reach out and say hi.

Zack Bernbaum

President & Producer

Zack Bernbaum is a Toronto based producer, and is the founder and president of Ezeqial Productions.

With an eye for thoughtful and imaginative stories, Bernbaum works with writers and production partners to create and develop projects for Canadian and worldwide marketplaces.

Bernbaum graduated with Honours from Ryerson University’s Image Arts Film Studies.
